The community says...

Any platform engineer today would recognise the value of Internal Developer Platforms (IDP) and how it has revolutionised the software space. As a long-time core platform developer as well as a data leader, Animesh recognised the value of the IDP standard and modeled the Data Developer Platform (DDP) Standard after it along with Travi Thompson and Shubhanshu Jain as founding contributors.

But software principles do not directly apply to the world of data. While the IDP model works beautifully for software that's static and code-dependent by nature, DDP was built to incorporate and look after the dynamic element of Data where change is the only constant and dependent on factors beyond the immediate control of data developers.

Within a short span, the DDP standard has become a community-driven initiative with practitioners implementing platforms after DDP, customising as necessary, and publishing their work and ideas.
